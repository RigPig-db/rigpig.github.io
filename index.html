SYSTEM_IDENTITY // LORE & COMBAT

Sci-Fi survival sandbox. Extraterrestrial contact as routine survival. PvP is the anomaly; PvE is the contract. Wealth through extraction and automation.

ALIEN INCURSION & FAUNA

  • Drop Pods: Fall from orbit; require pristine crowbar for breach. 4-stage wave containment combat protocols.
  • Dragons: Capable of ranged thermal attacks. Elimination yields a 5M drop; heads are commercial assets.
  • Drones & Synths: S&D Drones deploy mob swarms on destruction. Robo-Soldiers detonate upon elimination (extract CPUs/Heads with tools).
  • Aliens: Found in unknown sectors. Require specialized Alien Knife to dismember limbs for high-value trading.

WEAPON ENGINEERING

  • Forge Output: Kinetic damage for all custom forged weaponry is calibrated at 6M per impact.
  • Space Weapon Forge: Synthesizes advanced hardware by fusing Raw Artifacts with the R&M BeastMaster tool.
  • Hardware Upgrades: Recoil CPUs mitigate kickback (10%-100%). Heavenly Mag Loader automates empty magazines.
  • Rare Procurement: Mech Dino Rifle (Requires Skyrim Albums + Purple Mask). Mech Dragon Pistol (34 artifacts + 150M credits).

EXCLUSION_ZONES & DYNAMIC_EVENTS

Technology replacing instinct. Machines guarding what's left of us. Survival by design, not chance.

TERRITORIAL EVENTS

  • Event Claiming: Control over KOTH, Domes, or Arenas requires direct entrance claiming. Claim voids on squad wipe.
  • No-Fly Zones: Violating restricted airspace triggers a 10M fine. Landing results in vehicle seizure.
  • Chernarus Badlands: Absolute darkness. Restricted to 3 operators max (45-minute cycle limit).

VAULTS & CONTAINMENT

  • DNA Strongrooms: Accessed via cryptographic keycards. Breach triggers hostile mob deployments on the perimeter.
  • Containment Domes: No keycards. One-way doors, 3-player max, 30-40 min cooldown cycles.
  • Player Load: Personal inventory capped at 2000 slots. Exo-Skeletons bypass this. Stacking containers inside clothing is bannable.

SETTLEMENT ARCHITECTURE

BUILDING PROTOCOLS

  • Group Limits: 1 base & 1 flag per group. Max 5 members. Leaving official Discord = base wipe.
  • No-Build Zones: Strictly prohibited in towns, cities, quest areas, and orange map circles.
  • Perimeter: 1000m from Traders, 300m from military/events, 250m from existing bases.
  • Structural Limits: Max altitude is 4 levels (angled roofs count as full room). Messy/floating bases will be deleted.
  • Prefab Architecture: 1 base type allowed. BBP restricted to fencing/doors. Vanilla building prohibited.

FABRICATION & STORAGE

  • Storage Capacity: Hard-cap of 6000 slots per base. Bunkers have infinite storage but require claimed flag.
  • Hardware Quotas: Max 2 Solar Panels, 1 Crypto Miner, 1 3D Printer, and 1 of each Workbench per base.
  • Agriculture: Limited to 3 Greenhouses. Standard farm plots are disabled.

VEHICLE & SERVER PROTOCOLS

Vehicles left unparked outside virtual garages or packing wrenches will be permanently repossessed if unattended for 24 hours.

  • Release fee: 500,000 Credits.
  • Physics Stability: Vehicles must only be packed on completely flat terrain to prevent Enfusion engine glitches.
  • Exploit Penalties: Using vehicles as elevated platforms to farm mobs incurs a 50M fine and denial of repair services.
  • Admin Services: Requesting an admin to retrieve a dead bodybag costs a 10M in-game fee.
