CORE_DIRECTIVES // THE PVE CONTRACT

Welcome to the cluster, operator. We run a cooperative PvE network here. Keep the comms clean, respect your fellow survivors, and let's keep the grid running smoothly.

GENERAL CONDUCT

  • Respect the Loot: If it isn't yours, just walk past it. Leave it alone.
  • PvE Mandate: Intentionally shooting another player in anger is strictly prohibited. We're on the same side.
  • Disputes: Banter is great, but constant abuse in voice or chat isn't. If someone's bothering you, ask them to stop. If it continues, just open a ticket. Keep personal arguments off the server.

DATA LOGS & COMMS

  • Visual Proof: We highly recommend using software like Medal to record your sessions. Refunds and replacements can only be processed with visual evidence. Capture those bugs and crashes!
  • Comms Link: Staying in our official Discord is required. Disconnecting means your base and assets are considered abandoned and may be wiped without warning.
  • Need Backup? If you're unsure about anything, always ask an admin. We're here to help.

SETTLEMENT_ARCHITECTURE // BASE PROTOCOLS

ZONING & SPACING

  • Grid Spacing: Keep 1000m from any Trader. Stay 300m away from military zones, dynamic events, towns, and strongrooms. Give existing neighbors a 250m buffer.
  • No-Build Zones: Towns, cities, quest nodes, and orange map sectors are totally off-limits. Why? So no one gets a monopoly on loot respawns. Let's keep it fair!
  • Relocation: Building a new home? Finish it first, then dismantle the old one (or ask an admin to wipe it for you).

STRUCTURE CONSTRAINTS

  • Group Limits: 1 flag and 1 base per squad (max 5 operators). Got a bunker? You'll need 2 flags for that.
  • Architecture: Stick to 1 base type (Bunker, Treehouse, House Kit, etc.). 4 stories max height. Vanilla building is prohibited by default. You can use BBP, but only for fencing and doors.
  • Aesthetics: Furnish it however you like, but build it properly! Messy or thrown-together floating bases might get deleted.

HARDWARE & STORAGE

  • Storage Caps: 6000 total slots per base. Keep tabs on your usage. Stacking cases inside clothing to bypass limits is not permitted.
  • Displays & NBC: Gear stands are for display only. NBC cases are strictly for NBC gear. If abused, they'll be deleted without warning or refunds.
  • Utilities: Max 2 Solar Panels, 1 Crypto Miner (Rig or PC), 1 3D Printer, 3 Greenhouses (garden plots disabled), and 1 of each workbench.

FLEET_MANAGEMENT // VEHICLE PROTOCOLS

Vehicles are precious hardware. Please pack them away safely to keep server performance smooth for everyone. Remember: items stored inside are kept at your own risk.

  • Parking: Unattended vehicles left out for 24h+ might be repossessed (Repo release fee is 500k). Use the Phase Compressor to pack them into Mechanical Storage Crates, or use Virtual Garages.
  • Physics Warning: DayZ physics can be wild. Only pack, unpack, or exit vehicles (especially spacecraft!) on perfectly solid, flat ground to avoid getting launched off the map.
  • Exploit Warning: Using your vehicle as a vantage point for killing mobs disrupts the simulation. It carries a 50M credit fine, and we won't repair the damage.
  • Operator Responsibility: If you crash it, shoot it, drown it, or get it stuck, that's on you (10% repair fee, 500k retrieval fee). Server crashes or theft? We've got your back.
  • Ruined Vehicles: They despawn. Don't leave them unattended expecting them to stay. Replacement costs 20% of the purchase price.

OPERATIONS // EVENTS & MORTALITY

The wasteland is dangerous. Be smart, stay safe, and know the protocols when things go sideways.

SERVER CYCLES & RESTARTS

  • Safety Window: At least 5 minutes before a restart, please stop flying, driving, building, or transferring loot. Rollbacks can happen!
  • Death Near Restart: If you die right before a cycle reset, the server might not log it properly. Your body and items could be permanently lost, so secure yourself early.

BODYBAG RETRIEVALS

  • Self-Recovery: We expect you to try and recover your own drop first. It's part of the survival experience!
  • Admin Extraction: If the zone is too hot or impossible to reach, admins can retrieve your bodybag for a 10M in-game fee.
  • Tickets: Open a ticket immediately with the exact time log. If staff is offline, we'll hold your death info and retrieve it later when available.

EVENTS & RESTRICTED AIRSPACE

  • Event Claims: Claim KOTH, Arenas, or Domes in chat ONLY when you arrive at the entrance. If your squad wipes, it's fair game again. Please don't touch other players' bodies.
  • No-Fly Zones: We take restricted airspace seriously. Flying through incurs an instant 10M fine. Landing inside means your craft is seized (costs 50% to recover, and contents are lost).
